Oakes Lab

The Oakes Lab at Polytechnique Montréal researches knowledge engineering for complex cyber-physical systems and digital twins.

Objective: Accelerating Knowledge Engineering for Complex Systems

We start with the expert's domain knowledge, capture it in models, and then utilize it to better construct, optimize, verify, and validate complex systems.

Digital Twins Construction, reporting, and monitoring of DT systems for cyber-physical systems Systems Engineering Model-based systems engineering, MDE frameworks, and knowledge capturing for complex systems Machine Learning for Engineering Tasks ML and RL for fault injection, safety analysis, rationale mining, and model repair Knowledge Representation Ontologies, knowledge graphs, and semantic modelling to structure knowledge for complex systems
