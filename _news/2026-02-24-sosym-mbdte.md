Our paper [*Model-based Digital Twin Engineering: Insights, Challenges, and Future Directions*](https://steffen-zschaler.de/assets/pdf/sosym_mbdte_26.pdf) has been accepted at the Journal on Software and Systems Modelling (SoSyM). It was co-authored with Philipp Zech, Souvik Barat, Benjamin Nast, Judith Michael, Steffen Zschaler, Balbir Barn, and Ruth Breu.
